* = splitting field. SD, SFK, FSFK, and Parts each go on separate orders. Within SD/SFK/FSFK, unique combos of Species + Finish category + Glaze + Finish process split. SFK and FSFK additionally split by Drawer Front type (Slab vs 5PC). Atlantis, Aruba & Salem (SD/SFK) get their own orders. Parts split into three buckets: Paint Color Blocks (CBBOX, CBSET, CBSET-M, CBSET-PNT), Stain Color Blocks (CBSET-C, CBSET-H, CBSET-O, CBSET-WO, CBSET-STN), and general Parts (LIT, TKIT, etc.). CB lines ride along on matching SD/SFK orders when species + finish + glaze + finish process all match. Drag the bottom scrollbar to see all columns.
